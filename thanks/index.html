When Jesse first approached me about designing and implementing the user interface for a web application to help expedite the response times for remote on-the-job injuries.

I was immediately interested. The problem? A majority of workmans comp lawsuits had everything to do with unclear policies, slow communication, and missing accountability. Our goal was twofold, to build a platform to quickly and comprehensively alert the necessary parties when a on-the-job injury had occurred and provide a repository to save and securely share all the necessary information between physicians, managers, lawyers, human resources, insurance managers, and all other stakeholders involved.
